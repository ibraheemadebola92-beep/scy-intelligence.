FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does a project take? +
It depends on the project. Flyers take 1-2 days. Websites take 3-7 days. Apps and AI projects take 1-4 weeks. I'll give you an exact timeline after discussing your project.
How do I pay? +
We accept OPay, bank transfer, and other Nigerian payment methods. You pay 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. No international payments required.
Can I request revisions? +
Yes! Every project includes up to 3 free revisions. Additional revisions are available at a small extra cost.
Do you offer discounts? +
Yes! Students get 15% discount. Referrals earn you 10% off your next order. Bulk orders (3+ services) get 20% off.
What if I'm not satisfied? +
Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you're not happy after revisions, we'll discuss a refund or alternative solution. I want you to be 100% satisfied.
